Technical setup guide

Interview platform

We use Riverside.fm for remote interviews. It provides high-quality audio and video recording with separate tracks for host and guest. Simple browser-based interface, no downloads required.

Audio equipment

Apple microphones (including MacBook built-in) work well
External mics like Blue Yeti or Audio-Technica are great
Position microphone 6-8 inches from your mouth
Use wired headphones to prevent echo
Find a quiet environment with minimal background noise

Video & environment

Position camera at eye level (external webcam preferred)
Face a light source; avoid being backlit
Choose a clean, uncluttered background
Use a wired internet connection when possible
Find a quiet space with minimal echo
